Brother James M. Reed IV A Legacy of Leadership

Brother James M. Reed IV, (2-98-Xi Kapa Kappa), initiated on March 2nd, 1998. Brother Reed is married to Latoya T. Jones-Reed, Ph.D., they have two beautiful children Londyn and Lakyn Reed, and currently call Madison, MS home. Over 17 consecutive, active years, Bro. Reed has held fraternal leadership positions from the chapter level to the district level. As a chapter Basilius, First Vice State Representative (MS), and currently the Seventh District Stop the Violence Prevention Chairman, his leadership skills have proven beneficial to the overall mission of the fraternity.

As a former executive officer for the State of MS Organization, Bro. Reed has implemented effective and efficient change for the betterment of our beloved organization. During his tenure as the 7th District Stop the Violence Prevention Committee Chairman and also as an International Committee member, Bro. Reed has added valuable insight on effective approaches for dealing with internal and external violence that plagues our fraternity and the African American community at large.

Bro. Reed has been successful in his leadership experiences within the Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc., as well as with other organizations, this serves evident of his ability to make a significant impact on the way our records are kept and our seal is protected within the 7th District.
